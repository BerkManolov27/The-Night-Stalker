FINGER STALKER

Evade the stalker. Collect the coins.

Control the player with your index finger via webcam

How To Play

  • Press INITIALIZE GAME or press S to start.
  • Move your index finger to control the player.
  • Place your finger in the center circle to begin each level.
  • Collect all coins and avoid the enemy.
  • You have 3 hearts. Getting hit removes one heart.
  • Press F to pause, R to restart, and M to toggle music.
